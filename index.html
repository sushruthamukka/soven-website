Who We Are

A Result-Oriented Organisation

Soven Liaisoning Services was incorporated in 1992 and is currently based in Hyderabad. We have been associated with many multi-nationals for the successful completion of their projects in Telangana & Andhra Pradesh.

Before the liberalization of Government Policies, there was a great scarcity of experienced professionals with immense knowledge about local Regulatory & Statutory Rules. This led many Multi-national Organizations to step back or face delays in approvals — affecting their profitability and business opportunity.

"Taking advantage of Government policies requires not just knowledge, but the ability to leave a positive impression on government stakeholders. Only Seasoned and Intelligent Professionals can render these services."

What We Do

Comprehensive Liaisoning Solutions

SLS comprises experienced professionals in Urban Planning, Building Services, Environment and Regulatory Approvals. We serve Townships, Malls, Residential Towers, Hospitals, Industries, Pharma Projects, SEZs, IT Buildings and more.

🏗️

Pre-Construction & Development

Conversion of property to developmental status within zoning regulations. Change of land use from GHMC, HMDA, DTCP, IALA, MA & UD and other statutory authorities.

📋

For Construction & Development

Liaison between Proponents and Statutory Bodies. Coordination with Architects, scrutiny of drawings, preparing technical applications, arranging NOCs and Regulatory Clearances.

🏢

Post Construction

Occupancy Certificates from Fire & Emergency Services, Consent for Operation from Pollution Control Board, and certificates from all sanctioning authorities.

⚖️

Legal Liaison

Address legal issues and liaise with concerned authorities. Maintain complete details on current regulatory practices for Entrepreneurs, Investors and Companies.

🏭

Industrial Approvals

Factory Building Layout approvals, Factory Licenses, Director of Factories and Boilers compliance, periodic renewals and all routine industrial compliances.

🌐

Strategic Support

Strategic support in operating business and assisting in liaising with various government officials in clearing operational hindrances across Telangana & AP.
