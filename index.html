Who We Are

About Soven Liaison

Founded by M.Venkat in 1998 Soven Liaison is a professionally driven consultancy firm specializing in liaisoning services, real estate advisory, land coordination, project facilitation, and strategic business support across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

Built on decades of industry experience, we help developers, investors, businesses, and individual clients navigate complex regulatory and operational processes with clarity, efficiency, and professionalism. From land-related coordination and documentation to project facilitation and strategic business support, we are known for delivering reliable, transparent, and result-oriented solutions.

"At Soven Liaison, trust, integrity, and long-term relationships remain at the core of everything we do — enabling us to consistently support successful ventures through dependable guidance and seamless execution."
